Privacy Policy

Nexora Explorer

Last updated: 2026-04-19

English

This Privacy Policy explains how Nexora Explorer (the "App") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and only process data required for file browsing and transfer features.

1. Information We Process

  • SMB connection details you enter (such as host, username, domain, share settings).
  • SMB passwords are stored locally in your device Keychain.
  • Connection profile metadata may be stored locally in app storage (UserDefaults).
  • Files you browse, preview, upload, or download are processed for app functionality.

2. What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not operate our own cloud backend for account/profile syncing.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs.

3. Network and Local Network Access

The App requests Local Network permission to discover and connect to NAS/SMB servers in your LAN. Data transfer happens between your device and the server you choose to connect to.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties except as required by law, or when technically necessary to complete connections to servers you explicitly configure.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

  • You can remove saved SMB profiles from inside the App at any time.
  • Deleting the App removes local app data from your device (subject to iOS behavior).

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will handle the request appropriately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: support@nexora.app

简体中文

本隐私政策说明 Nexora Explorer（以下简称“本应用”）如何处理您的信息。 我们重视您的隐私，仅处理实现文件浏览与传输功能所必需的数据。

1. 我们处理的信息

  • 您主动输入的 SMB 连接信息（如主机地址、用户名、域、共享设置）。
  • SMB 密码仅保存在您设备本地的 Keychain 中。
  • 连接配置的元数据可能保存在本地应用存储（UserDefaults）中。
  • 您浏览、预览、上传或下载的文件会在实现功能时被处理。

2. 我们不做的事情

  • 我们不运营用于账号/配置同步的自建云后端。
  • 我们不会出售您的个人信息。
  • 我们不使用广告 SDK。

3. 本地网络权限说明

本应用申请“本地网络”权限，用于发现并连接您局域网内的 NAS/SMB 服务器。 数据传输发生在您的设备与您主动配置连接的服务器之间。

4. 数据共享

除非法律法规要求，或为完成您明确发起的服务器连接所必需， 我们不会向第三方共享您的数据。

5. 数据保留与删除

  • 您可随时在应用内删除已保存的 SMB 配置。
  • 卸载应用后，设备本地应用数据将被移除（以 iOS 实际行为为准）。

6. 儿童隐私

本应用不专门面向 13 岁以下儿童。如您认为儿童向我们提供了个人信息， 请联系我们，我们将依法处理。

7. 政策更新

我们可能不时更新本政策。更新后会在本页面发布，并更新“最后更新日期”。

8. 联系方式

如您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请联系： support@nexora.app
